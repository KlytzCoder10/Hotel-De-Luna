frequently asked questions

What makes Hotel De Luna unique compared to other hotels?

Hotel De Luna offers a supernatural experience inspired by the hit KDrama series. Our hotel blends the ordinary with the extraordinary, providing a unique stay where ghosts find closure and guests encounter the mystical.

How can I book a room at Hotel De Luna?

Booking a room is simple! Visit our reservation page, choose your preferred dates, select a room, and complete the booking process. Confirmation details will be sent to your email.

Are the staff at Hotel De Luna real ghosts?

Our website is themed around the KDrama, and while our staff isn’t made up of actual ghosts, we strive to provide an enchanting and memorable experience that captures the spirit of the show.

Can I visit Hotel De Luna during the day?

Yes, you can book and visit the hotel any time of day. While our website plays on the mystical elements of the series, we are open 24/7 for your convenience.

What types of rooms are available at Hotel De Luna?

We offer a variety of uniquely themed rooms, including the Moonlight Suite, Eclipse Chamber, Spectral Haven, Phantom's Retreat, Starlight Sanctuary, and Ghostly Quarters. Each room is designed to provide a unique and enchanting experience.
